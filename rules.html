These basic guidelines apply, and will be enforced for the protection of our guests and the public property:

  1. Motor vehicles must only be operated on streets or parking areas.
  2. Speed with in the park is to be 5 MPH or less.
  3. All campers shall register at the office.
  4. Check out time is 12:00 noon. Check in time is 1:30pm.
  5. Camp site shall facilitate 1 camping apparatus (tent, trailer, motorhome) one vehicle and up to 6 people. Additional vehicles are $5.00 a day and boat launch is $5.00 a day.
  6. Each campsite is permitted 2 domestic animals, which shall be limited to dogs and cats.
    Each must be kept on a leash shorter than 8 feet at all times. Park management in his or her sole judgment may disallow any pet that disturbs the peace and tranquility of the park.
  7. No horses are permitted in the park.
  8. Camping is not permitted on the beach.
  9. Wood cutting is not permitted on the beach.
  10. Driving is not permitted on the beach.
  11. Loaded firearms are not permitted in the Park or on the beach.
  12. Fireworks are not permitted in the Park or on the beach.
  13. Use of loud speakers is not permitted in the Park.
  14. No soliciting is permitted in the Park.
  15. Public intoxication and or is disorderly conduct is prohibited.
  16. Quiet Hours 10p.m. -7a.m.

These rules have been established by ordinance and any violation shall be constitute a misdemeanor which will be enforced as necessary by Wahkiakum County law enforcement officers.
